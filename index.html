It's Easy and Smart

Another-F*ing-slider.js is easy to setup and configure. Try it in your project.

Browser Support

This plugin was was tested and works on Chrome, Safari, Firefox and IE9 & above.

Foolproof Implementation

$('#slide-container').anotherFuckingSlider();

Easy to adjust

$('#slide-container').anotherFuckingSlider({  
    speed:          500,
    controlsID:     'afs-controls',
    nextID:         'afs-next',
    prevID:         'afs-prev',
    autoRotate:     true,
    rotateTime:     10000,
    activeClass:    'active'
});
